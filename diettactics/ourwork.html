The Secret Weight loss tips Exposed!
Weight Loss tips #1

Drink Mainly Water
A energy or sports drink, beer, or fruit smoothies — each serving contains about 100 calories. Yet these beverages don't satisfy you the way 100 calories of food does, so they're a waste. Other liquids may be high in sodium and carbohydrates, which trick your body into retaining water, puffing you out.

Exception: One to 2 glasses of dry red wine per night is no problem.

Water, on the other hand, has zero carbs and calories, zero!  and little to no sodium, making it the perfect slim-down drink. And insanely, it actually assists in flushing out excess water weight and it jump-starts your metabolism. If it's just to plain, add mint leaves or yellow lemon wedges.

<strong>Do Cardio 30 Minutes a Day</strong>

Any workout that gets your heart rate up will burn calories. But you'll use more calories if you pick a cardio routine that engages multiple muscles simultaneously, says Wendy Larkin, personal-training manager at Crunch's Polk Street gym, in San Francisco.

Three to consider: boot-camp workouts, spinning, and cardio kickboxing,  At least 10min of each torches 200 to 300 calories while toning up your arms, legs, and core. You will notice everything appears sleeker and tighter.

You'll burn even more calories per session if your workout contains: alternating short bursts of intense cardio with slower activity. Experts aren't sure why it works, but many experience trainers swear by it.

A tactic that will make you burn fat the fastest:  Right when you get up in the morning, do 50 <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aerobic_exercise">aerobic</a> jumping jacks and 20 pushups. The workout combination is deadly. By engaging both the cardiovascular and muscular systems first thing in the morning without food in the body, you cause your body to burn tons of stored fat.

This is the one exception to the stick-to-water-only rule: Just as a coffee run makes your morning at work more productive, a pre-exercise cup of java with a splash of skim milk (about 11 calories) or black (just 5 calories) will energize your workout, explains Dr. Klauer<i>.</i>"You'll burn more calories without realizing you're pushing yourself harder."




Weight loss tips #4
Get an extra 30 minutes of beauty rest.

That extra half an hour, whether you sleep 5 hours or 8, can refresh you enough that you will make better food choices (in other words, no quick sugar fix for breakfast in search of energy) and won't feel lethargic and skip the gym says dietician experts. More restful sleep (7 to 8 hours is best) also boosts your metabolism. If you dont have the extra 30 minutes to sleep, another great weight loss tip is to take a 5 minute power nap which is used to supplement sleep.  And since your body builds muscle while you sleep, getting some shut eye will amount in better muscle tone..
